# 10. FAQ

Quick answers to the questions people ask most often.

## Getting started

### Do I need to know how to code to use Codapac?

No. Codapac is built for people who **can't** or **don't want to** write
code. If you can describe what you want in plain English, you can ship.

### Is there a free version?

Yes — you can sign up, create projects, and use your agent squad without
paying. Paid plans add higher limits and team features.

### What browsers does Codapac work in?

Any modern browser: Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari, Arc, Brave. Codapac
works on phones and tablets too, though the project page is easier to use
on a laptop-sized screen.

## Projects

### How many projects can I have?

As many as you want. Archive the ones you're not using to keep your
Projects page tidy.

### I don't see my project — where did it go?

Check:

1. The **Archived** filter on the Projects page.
2. The **search bar** — try a word from the project name.
3. The account you're signed in with (top-right avatar). You might be
   signed in with a different email.

### Can I rename a project?

Yes. Open the project → click the **"..."** menu in the top bar → **Rename**.

### Can I delete a project?

Yes, from the same **"..."** menu → **Delete project**. This is
permanent — chat history, cards, and previews all go with it.

## Chat and agents

### The agent is stuck — what do I do?

Three things to try:

1. Send a gentle nudge: *"Are you stuck? What do you need from me?"*
2. Refresh the page. The squad picks up exactly where they were.
3. If a specific card has been sitting too long, move it back to **To
   Do** from the card menu — your squad will retry.

### Can I undo what the squad did?

Often, yes. In chat, say *"Undo the last change"* or *"Revert CP-142."*
Your squad will restore the previous version.

### Can I pause the squad?

Yes. From the project's **"..."** menu, click **Pause project**. The
squad will stop picking up new cards until you **Resume**.

### Who can see my chat?

Anyone with access to the project. By default, that's just you. If you
invite teammates, they'll see the same chat and board.

## Board and tasks

### Can I move a card back a lane?

Yes. Hover the card for the arrow buttons, or open the card and use the
lane picker at the top.

### Can I assign a card to a specific agent?

Yes — when creating an issue via **New issue**, pick the agent. You can
also say in chat: *"Assign CP-142 to TESTEES."*

### How do I add tags?

When creating an issue, type tags separated by commas or spaces (up to
four). You can also say *"tag CP-142 as mobile and polish"* in chat.

## Preview and sharing

### Who can see my live preview?

By default, the preview link is public — anyone with the URL can open
it, but the link is long and not listed publicly.

### How do I make my project private?

Open the project → **"..."** menu → **Visibility** → **Private**. Only
signed-in members of the project can see the preview.

### Can I point my own domain at the preview?

On paid plans, yes — ask your squad: *"Connect my domain
example.com to this project."*

## Account

### Is my work saved?

Yes, automatically, every few seconds. You can close the tab any time.

### How do I sign out on all devices?

Open **Profile** → **Security** → **Sign out everywhere**. You'll need
to sign back in anywhere you want to use Codapac.

### I forgot which email I used.

Try both. Codapac always uses email sign-in, so whichever address you
try, if it's not registered, you'll just see *"No account found."*

## Something's not in this list

Ask your squad in any project chat: *"How do I do X in Codapac?"* —
they'll either tell you directly or point you to the right place.
