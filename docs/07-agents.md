# 7. Your agent squad

This page introduces the three agents who do the work with you in every project.

Codapac ships with a built-in **squad** of three agents. They collaborate
behind the scenes the same way a small, well-run product team would —
planner, builder, and tester.

You don't need to pick who answers. They sort it out between themselves
based on what you asked.

## Meet the squad

### BOSS — the planner

- **What BOSS does:** reads your message, shapes it into clear tasks
  with done-criteria, and drops them on the board in **To Do**.
- **BOSS is your friend when:** you're starting something new, your ask
  is big or fuzzy, you want to prioritize, or you need a decision.
- **Talk to BOSS like:** *"I want a landing page for a dog-walking
  service. Plan it out."*

### FIXER — the builder

- **What FIXER does:** picks up cards from **To Do**, does the actual
  work, and moves the card to **Done** with notes on what changed.
- **FIXER is your friend when:** the plan exists and you want it built,
  or you want something tweaked, renamed, swapped, or removed.
- **Talk to FIXER like:** *"Please change the hero headline to 'Walks that
  wag'."*

### TESTEES — the tester

- **What TESTEES does:** checks each card in **Done** — tries the
  happy path, pokes at edge cases, looks on mobile — then either signs
  off (the card moves to **Merged**) or sends it back with a list of
  problems.
- **TESTEES is your friend when:** you want a thorough second set of eyes,
  or you suspect something's broken but can't pin it down.
- **Talk to TESTEES like:** *"Can you double-check the sign-up flow on
  mobile?"*

## Reading squad status (the "orb" and badges)

Next to each card — and in the top of the chat — your squad members show
a small status. You'll see:

- **Idle** — ready to help, nothing in progress.
- **Working** — currently on a card. You'll often see a gentle pulse
  animation.
- **Waiting on you** — they asked a question or need your review.
- **Blocked** — something stopped them; they'll explain in chat.

If you're curious what each agent is good at, open **Agents** from the top
bar (when signed out) — you'll see a profile page with each agent's
skills and personality.

## Tips for working with the squad

- **Trust the handoffs.** You rarely need to specify *who* does what. If
  you say *"Add a pricing page and make sure it works on mobile,"* BOSS
  plans, FIXER builds, TESTEES checks — all without you tagging anyone.
- **Override when it matters.** If you know exactly who you want, say so:
  *"TESTEES, please re-check CP-142."*
- **Give feedback in normal language.** *"Love it"* or *"This font feels
  off — try something more modern"* is enough.
- **Stuck? Ask the squad itself.** *"What's blocking you?"* and they'll
  tell you.

## If something goes wrong

- **"The squad is quiet."** Send a message — they only start once there's
  something to do.
- **"Wrong agent replied."** Reassign from the card, or just say who you
  want: *"BOSS, can you take this one?"*
- **"TESTEES keeps bouncing the card back."** Open the card — the notes
  explain what's failing. Either ask FIXER to tackle those specific
  points, or say *"Ship it anyway — we'll address those later."*
