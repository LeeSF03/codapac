# 1. Getting started

This page helps you sign in, take a first look around, and get ready to start your first project.

## Sign in

1. Open **codapac.app** in your browser.
2. Click **Sign in** in the top-right corner.
3. You'll see a sign-in card. Pick one of:
   - **Continue with Google** — one click, you're in.
   - **Email** — type your email, click **Send code**, then type the
     6-digit code we email you and click **Verify**.
4. The first time you sign in, Codapac will ask you to choose a **username**.
   This is how you appear to teammates. Pick one and click **Continue**.

That's it — you're in. You'll land on your **Projects** page.

## What's on the top bar

The top bar is always visible. From left to right:

- The **Codapac logo** — click it to go home.
- **Projects** — the list of every project you have access to.
- **Your avatar** (top right) — open your profile, change the theme (light/dark),
  or sign out.

## The Projects page

This is your home base. Every project you create or are added to lives here.

- Each project is a card with its **name**, a short **description**, an
  **emoji**, and a status dot (Active, Paused, or Archived).
- The **search bar** at the top filters by name.
- The **filters** let you switch between **All**, **Starred**, and **Archived**.
- The **sort** menu lets you order by **Last updated**, **Recently created**,
  or **Name**.
- Click the **star** on any card to pin a project to the top.

## Start your first project

When you're ready:

1. Click the big **New project** button on the Projects page.
2. Jump to the next guide: [Creating a project](./02-creating-a-project.md).

## If something goes wrong

- **The email code didn't arrive.** Check your spam folder, wait 30 seconds,
  and click **Resend**. Double-check the email address.
- **"Username taken."** Try another one — usernames are unique across Codapac.
- **Stuck on the sign-in page after clicking the link?** Refresh the page
  and try again. Your session should pick up where it left off.
