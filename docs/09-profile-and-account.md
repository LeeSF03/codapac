# 9. Profile and account

This page covers your personal settings — name, avatar, theme, and signing out.

## Opening your profile

1. Click your **avatar** in the top-right of the top bar.
2. In the menu, click **Profile**.

You can also visit **codapac.app/profile** directly once signed in.

## What's on the profile page

- **Avatar** — your picture. Click it to upload a new one (or remove it to
  go back to the default initial-circle).
- **Display name** — how you appear across Codapac (on comments, activity
  logs, and to teammates).
- **Username** — your unique handle. It's part of your profile URL.
- **Email** — the address you sign in with. Shown to you only.
- **Theme** — switch between **Light**, **Dark**, and **System** (follows
  whatever your computer is set to).

Everything saves automatically as you type. A small "Saved" confirmation
pops up.

## Changing the theme

Two ways:

1. From **Profile**, pick **Light**, **Dark**, or **System**.
2. Or, use the **theme button** in the top bar (sun/moon icon). This is
   the same setting — they stay in sync.

## Changing your username

You can change it later if you want, but keep in mind:

- Your old username will free up for others after a short while.
- Any links using your old profile URL will stop working.

Type a new one in the **Username** field. Codapac will tell you if it's
available.

## Signing out

1. Click your **avatar** in the top bar.
2. Click **Sign out** at the bottom of the menu.

You'll land back on the Codapac homepage. Your projects are still saved
— sign back in any time.

## Deleting your account

If you want to permanently remove your account:

1. Open **Profile**.
2. Scroll to **Danger zone** at the bottom.
3. Click **Delete account** and confirm.

This removes your account, your projects, and your chat history. It can't
be undone.

## Tips

- **Pick a clear display name.** Teammates see this on every activity
  entry and comment — real names beat *"user_8392"*.
- **System theme** is a good default. It picks up whatever your phone or
  computer is using.
- **Keep your email current.** That's how we send sign-in codes when
  you're logged out.

## If something goes wrong

- **"My avatar won't upload."** The file might be too big. Try an image
  under 5 MB in JPEG or PNG format.
- **"I can't change my username."** Someone else has it. Try a variation.
- **"I deleted my account by mistake."** Contact support from the
  homepage — we can sometimes recover very recent deletions.
