# Glossary

Every Codapac word, in plain English.

### Activity feed

The running log of what your squad just did, shown inside a project.
Think of it as a news ticker.

### Agent

One of the three members of your squad — **BOSS**, **FIXER**, or
**TESTEES**. Agents work on your project for you and reply in the chat.

### Archived (project)

A project you're done with but don't want to delete. It's hidden from the
main list until you set the filter to **Archived**.

### Board

The left panel inside a project — the lanes and cards that show every
piece of work in flight.

### BOSS

The **planner** of your squad. Reads your request, turns it into clear
tasks with done-criteria, and drops them on the board.

### Card

A single piece of work on the board — a task, a fix, a polish. Also
called an **issue** or **task** in Codapac; they all mean the same thing.

### Card ID

The short code on every card (e.g. `CP-142`). Use it when referring to a
card in chat: *"Ship CP-142."*

### Codapac

The product you're reading about. A workspace where non-technical people
plan, build, and ship software with an AI squad.

### Dark mode

A theme with a dark background. Switch it from your profile or the
theme icon in the top bar.

### Done (lane)

The third lane on the board. **FIXER** finished the work; **TESTEES** is
about to check it.

### FIXER

The **builder** of your squad. Picks up cards and actually makes the
changes.

### Issue

Another word for **card** or **task** — one clear thing that needs to
happen.

### Kanban

A style of board with lanes that work flows through from left to right.
Codapac's board is kanban-style.

### Lane

A column on the board: **To Do**, **In Progress**, **Done**, or
**Merged**.

### Live preview

A real, working version of your project you can open in a browser tab —
usually at an address like `codapac.app/project/your-slug`. Updates
automatically as cards get merged.

### Merged (lane)

The last lane. The card has been tested, approved, and is now part of
your project.

### Pause (project)

Stops your squad from picking up new cards on this project. Resume it any
time from the project menu.

### Preview

Short for **live preview** (see above). Also used for per-card previews
— a link to review just that one change before it's merged.

### Project

A single workspace — one chat, one board, one live preview. You can have
as many projects as you want.

### Project chat

The shared conversation on the right side of a project page. Scoped to
that one project.

### QA

*Quality assurance*. The automatic checks TESTEES runs on every card
when it reaches **Done**.

### Squad

Your group of three agents — BOSS, FIXER, and TESTEES — working together
on a project.

### Slug

The short, URL-friendly version of your project's name, used in the
address bar. Example: `acme-landing`.

### Starred (project)

A project you've pinned to the top of your Projects list by clicking the
star.

### Status dot

The small coloured circle next to a project name. Green = active, amber
= paused, grey = archived.

### System theme

A theme option that follows whatever your computer is set to (light or
dark).

### Tag

A small coloured label on a card, like `mobile`, `bug`, or `copy`. Used
to group and filter cards.

### Task

Another word for **card** or **issue**.

### TESTEES

The **tester** of your squad. Checks each card in **Done**, flags
problems, and signs off when everything works.

### To Do (lane)

The first lane on the board — cards waiting to be picked up.

### Top bar

The strip along the top of every page with the logo, navigation, and
your avatar.

### Username

Your unique handle. Part of your profile URL, and how teammates see you.

### Visibility

Whether your project's live preview is **Public** (anyone with the link)
or **Private** (only signed-in members). Change it from the project menu.

### Workspace

A general word for where your project lives — the combination of chat,
board, activity feed, and preview you see on a project page.
